Events and Catering!

Whether you’re a larger party celebrating or a company planning an event, you’re more than welcome at Hospitable. We also offer catering if you’d like to host an event at home. Offsite event catering

We also take Hospitable on the road! We’ll come to you and can cater events of all sizes and occasions. We create custom menus for weddings, private dinners, corporate events, and cocktail parties - bringing the best of Hospitable straight to your table.

Choose the style of event you want, and we’ll make it fun. We tailor our menus to your event and are happy to accommodate dietary restrictions with advance notice. Reach out to us for booking or if you want to know more.
