About Us

Hospitable Stockholm is a cozy, family-style restaurant created by two lifelong friends Amy and Isabel with years of experience in the restaurant world. Our focus is on delicious food, great wine, and a warm, friendly atmosphere, all while keeping sustainability at the heart of what we do.

We work with farmers and importers who share our sustainable values, and our menu changes daily to reflect the seasons and available ingredients. Every dish is crafted from scratch, inspired by traditional Swedish comfort food with influences from across Europe.

Come by for a carefully crafted cocktail at the bar with some of our classic snacks, or enjoy a full evening at our “Spritbordet,” where we pair each dish with playful cocktails for a fun, interactive dining experience. Creativity, craftsmanship, and care are at the center of everything we do, making every visit special.
