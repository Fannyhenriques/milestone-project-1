About Us

Hospitable Stockholm is a cozy, family-style restaurant created by two lifelong friends with years of experience in the restaurant world. Our focus is on delicious food, great wine, and a warm, friendly atmosphere, all while keeping sustainability at the heart of what we do.

Our farmers and importers share our sustainable values, reflected in our daily-changing menu that follows the seasons and available ingredients. Every dish is crafted from scratch, with influences from across Europe with a modern touch.

Come by for a carefully crafted cocktail in the lounge, or enjoy a full evening at our bar-table, where we pair each dish with playful cocktails for a fun, interactive dining experience. Creativity, craftsmanship, and care are at the center of everything we do, making every visit special.
