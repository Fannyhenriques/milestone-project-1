Welcome!

Welcome to Hospitable Stockholm! We are two childhood friends sharing a passion for great food, fine wine, and a warm, neighborly atmosphere. Whether you want to make a reservation or just drop by for a drink in the bar, we’re here for you.

Philosophy

Our goal is to make everyone feel welcome and give you a truly memorable experience, celebrating thoughtful cuisine, warm hospitality, and shared moments. Come by for an after-work drink, a quick meal, or a leisurely dinner in our dining room, located in the heart of Stockholm.

Our food

Our food is inspired by the rich culinary traditions of Europe, reimagined with a modern and creative touch. We aim to blend classic flavors with contemporary techniques to offer a dining experience that feels both familiar and exciting.

Sustainability

We work with farmers and importers who share our sustainable mindset, and our menu changes daily according to the season and availability. Ensuring we deliver meals that are both high-quality and sustainably sourced.
