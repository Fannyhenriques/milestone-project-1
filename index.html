Welcome!

Welcome to Hospitable Stockholm! We are two childhood friends sharing a passion for great food, fine wine, and a warm, neighborly atmosphere. Whether you want to make a reservation or just drop by for a drink in the bar, we’re here for you.

We work with farmers and importers who share our sustainable mindset, and our menu changes daily according to the season and availability. Our goal is to make everyone feel welcome and give you a truly memorable experience in the heart of Stockholm.
